Physics and AI Workshop

The Department of Physics, RQMP and CAMBAM are sponsoring a workshop, it'll feature interesting speakers who are leading researchers in the use of machine learning in the natural sciences (Roger Melko from the University of Waterloo and Edward Ott from the University of Maryland).

There is ample of opportunity for students and postdocs in all physical sciences ( not only physics!) to give short talks and poster presentations.

Each day will have a dedicated hands-on workshop featuring introductory and intermediate level machine learning, which will be run by an expert in the topic from the McGill High Performance Computing Centre.

May 6-7. New Residence Hall. [Link]
